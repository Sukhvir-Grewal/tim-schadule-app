Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tim Schedule App (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services. This policy is applicable to all users of the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses certain information from your Google account, including:

  • Your Google Calendar data (to add events based on your schedule)
  • Your Gmail data (to fetch emails from specific senders for scheduling purposes)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to provide the following services:

  • Add schedule events to your Google Calendar automatically based on emails received
  • Ensure that no duplicate events are added to your calendar

Your data is only used for the purposes of providing these services and is not shared with third parties except as required by law.

3. Data Storage and Security

The App does not store your Google user data on our servers. All data access and processing happen on your local device. Your authorization token is stored securely in your browser's local storage to maintain your session and avoid repeated logins.

4. Sharing of Information

Your Google user data is not shared with any third parties. The App only uses your data to provide the services described above.

5. Compliance with Google's Limited Use Requirements

We strictly adhere to Google's Limited Use Requirements, meaning that your data will only be used for the features described in this Privacy Policy and as permitted by Google's policies.

6. In-Product Privacy Notifications

We provide in-product privacy notifications within the App interface to ensure that you are aware of how your data is used. These notifications are displayed prominently to ensure transparency.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.
